Support

If you need help with Jaita Tuner or want to report a bug, please use one of the options below.

Contact

Email us at jaita.tuner@gmail.com with a description of the problem and your device model.

Report a Bug

You can also open an issue on the GitHub repository.

Tips

  • Grant microphone permission when prompted — the tuner cannot detect pitch without it.
  • For best results, tune in a quiet environment.
  • Hold the device close to the chanter, away from the drones.
