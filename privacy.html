Privacy Policy

Jaita Tuner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Microphone

The app uses the device microphone solely to detect pitch in real time. Audio is processed on-device and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking services.

Network Access

The app does not connect to the internet. It works entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs or services.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please email jaita.tuner@gmail.com.
